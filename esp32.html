ESP32-WROOM-32E Pinout

Quick Notes

  • 3V3 = regulated 3.3V out / VCC rail
  • EN = chip enable/reset (active high)
  • GPIO34–39 are input-only (no PWM).

I²C (typ.)

  • SCL: GPIO22
  • SDA: GPIO21

Analog

  • ADC1: 36, 39, 34, 35, 32, 33
  • ADC2: 4, 12–15, 25–27
  • DAC: 25 (DAC1), 26 (DAC2)
